This app is pitched at long term investors who are interested in seeing performance returns for 1 or more stocks over a 10 year period

Currently, the stocks are confined to the UK FTSE 100 only

The information is pulled from Yahoo Finance. Please verify the data for yourself

The Plot provided shows the stock return for each year (up to 10 years) between:-

  • Close on the last trading date
  • Close on the last trading date 1 year, 2 year....10 years prior

    • The stock return for a specific period is calculated as "Stock price at close on last trading date - Stock price at close on last trading date x years prior/Stock price at close on last trading date x years prior

    For example, if JD Sports was priced at 148.2 at close of business on 24th November 2023 and was priced at 125.70 at close of business on 28th November 2022 then it's 1 year return is calculated as

    JD Sports 1 year return = 100*((148.2-125.7)/125.7) = 17.90%
