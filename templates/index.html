This website is a proof of concept pitched at long term investors who are interested in seeing performance returns for 1 or more stocks over a max 25 year period

Currently, the stocks are confined to the UK FTSE 100, DAX and the S&P500 stocks only. The information is pulled from Yahoo Finance.


From the menu you will find the following links:-

  • Plot plots the cumulative stock return for each year (up to 25 years) e.g 10 years = the percentage increase since latest trading days and last trading day 10 years ago.
    Sortable tables are also provided for the cumulative and year on year returns
    If you login (using Google Authentication), you are also able to save your stocks
  • Reports provides pre-canned reports which might be of use to an investor. Currently allows you to view the cumulative stock returns (for all included stocks) over x years
  • SQL allows an authenticated user to write their own queries and obtain resultsets (either displayed as searchable, sortable html tables or downloadable as CSV)
  • Blog provides help information for the website and articles that may assist long term investors in identifying high quality stocks
  • Legal provides legal terms & conditions
  • Chat has been trained on the Blog articles and you can ask it for any info contained within using natural language
