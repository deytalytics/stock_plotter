The reports provide data for FTSE 100, DAX and S&P 500 stocks only. Choose from one of these pre-canned reports or write your own SQL.

  • In past years, how much has the stock price risen in total?
    This is really a past popularity report. The past unfortunately does not influence the future, and the largest losses report shows that very popular stocks can fall dramatically
  • In past years, how many years produced positive returns?
    Stocks with more positive years give a reassuring feeling that they're less risky. But the largest daily losses report again shows that it can still be a wild ride.
  • What have been the largest daily gains & losses per stock and when did they occur?
    Note: Some of the large gains/losses look like they're due to glitches in the exchange data - you can verify this on Yahoo finance by clicking through on the stock symbol and looking at what happened near the reported date.
  • When was the stock price high and how high was it?
    This option can also give you a feel for the mood of investors if there haven't been any highs recently.
Note: If you go to the Plot menu option you can select your stocks and view Cumulative and Year on Year returns up to 25 years ago.
